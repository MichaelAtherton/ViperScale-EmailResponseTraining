# Track Inquiry — Response Pattern

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Customer Question Pattern
"I'm interested in buying a track"
"I saw you on Facebook, I want a track"
"Can I talk to someone about tracks?"
"I'm new here and need advice"

## Response Pattern
- **Route to Dan** — these are high-value sales ($700+)
- Dan prefers email over phone: "if they sent me an email, I could respond and then it's documented"
- For phone calls: Dan wants post-call recap email sent to customer with summary and relevant links
- Include links to: track assembly videos, YouTube playlist, "what's in your Viper track box" content

## Important
- These customers are often "giddy, happy, excited" — they want hand-holding through the process
- Dan handles these personally — do NOT try to close a track sale without Dan's involvement
- A holding response is fine: acknowledge receipt, provide relevant resource links, let them know Dan will follow up
