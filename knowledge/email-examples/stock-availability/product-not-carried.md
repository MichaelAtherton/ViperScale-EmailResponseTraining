# Product Not Carried — Response Pattern

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Customer Question Pattern
"Where can I find [product that Viper doesn't stock]?"
"Do you carry [OEM/vintage part]?"
"I can't find [product] on your website"

## Response Pattern
- For OEM/vintage parts: "best bet's going to be eBay"
- For items simply not in catalog: "here's a link to all of our products for that particular car. And if you don't see it, unfortunately, we don't have it"
- If Tyco-related: provide link to Tyco offerings as alternative

## Key Examples
- **Magnum 44X2 production wheels** → "best bet's going to be eBay"
- **OEM/reproduction front wheels** → "eBay or a link to our Tyco offerings"
