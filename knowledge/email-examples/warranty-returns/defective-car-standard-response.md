# Defective Car — Standard Response Pattern

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Customer Question Pattern
"Car ran fine for 50 laps, then went slower and never got back up to speed"
"My car isn't working right"
"I think I got a defective [product]"

## Response Pattern
Standard response: "send it back to attention repairs" with the shop mailing address.

Dan evaluates the car himself when received. He can tell from wear patterns whether it's a defect or user damage.

## Important Notes
- A car that fails at 50 laps has something different — Dan says they "will go like 10,000 laps before physically wearing out"
- **Never promise a specific resolution before Dan inspects the product**
- Dan will still take care of the customer even if they caused damage, but adjusts his response accordingly
- For wrong tires/parts on a car: either send correct parts or send replacement car + return label
