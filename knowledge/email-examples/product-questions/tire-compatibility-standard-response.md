# Tire Compatibility — Standard Response Pattern

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Customer Question Pattern
"What front/rear tires are recommended for the [chassis type]?"
"Where can I find replacement tires for the [car]?"
"What tires fit my [car type]?"

## Response Pattern
1. Look up the car type
2. Navigate to Shop by Car Type → [type] → Tires
3. Provide direct links to compatible products

Dan's approach: "I would just give him the links directly. Like, here's the SKUs, here's the links. Make it as easy as possible for him."

## Example — Magnet Traction Tires
> "The only options we have for rear tires are these two. And then when it comes to front tires, we only have one SKU, which is the 405 front end."

## Example — 4V1 Chassis Tires
> Give the stock size tires (what comes on the car) with SKUs and direct links

## Example — Production Modified Pricing
> "They range from $74.95 to $92.95" — search store for "production modified"
