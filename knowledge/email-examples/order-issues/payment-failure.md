# Payment Failure — Response Pattern

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Customer Question Pattern
"My payment didn't go through"
"My order was canceled"
"I can't complete my purchase"

## Response Pattern
- Common causes: maxed out card, insufficient funds, bank blocks unknown merchant
- Dan's approach: "try again with a different payment method"
- Alternative: "we can invoice him directly"
- For large orders (tracks): deposit arrangements possible (case by case)

## Notes
- Klarna and Affirm financing available but customers sometimes can't get approved
- Direct invoicing is a real option Dan uses
