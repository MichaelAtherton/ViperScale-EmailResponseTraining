# Car Chassis Guide

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## AFX Magnet Traction
- A specific car type/chassis in the AFX line
- Has limited tire options (only 2 rear, 1 front SKU)
- Has its own section on the website with ~23 results
- Armatures are available (despite search issues with specific terms vs "pancake armature")

## AFX Pancake Chassis/Armature
- "Pancake armature" is a search term customers use
- Viper DOES carry armatures for AFX pancake chassis
- Search issues: customers search specific armature names and can't find them; searching "pancake armature" or just browsing the category works better

## Mega G+ Chassis
- Customers want to put "big little rims" on Mega G+ cars
- Currently requires buying a complete assembly, pulling rims and tires off, and putting other ones on
- Dan wants to make a standalone rear axle part for this — "I just need to make the rear axle for that"

## 4V1 Chassis
- Used for "old AFX" cars
- Customer asked about front/rear tire recommendations
- Dan's approach: give stock size tires (what comes on the car) with SKUs and direct links

## Production Modified
- A car type/class
- Price range: $74.95 to $92.95
- Info is on the website
- Customers frequently ask about cost; search store for "production modified" and give the price range

## Cortin / Core 10
- "Cortin" is a sponsor name for a car (not a brand or type)
- "Core 10" may be how it's referenced; customer misspelled as "Cortin"
- Common for customers to misspell product names

## Chassis Interchangeability Notes
- The timing system is a **universal part** — works on Viper tracks, AFX track, any track
- Website is organized by car type to make compatibility clear: Shop by Car Type → [type] → category
