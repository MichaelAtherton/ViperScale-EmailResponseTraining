# Tire Compatibility Rules

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## AFX Magnet Traction Tires
- **Only two rear tire options exist** for Magnet Traction cars
- Neither rear tire listing specifies a size explicitly
- **SKU 420** — Rear tires (show up in search; Dan considers removing them from the Magnet Traction category to reduce confusion)
- **SKU 405** — Front end (the only front tire/wheel option for Magnet Traction): "when it comes to front tires, we only have one SKU, which is the 405 front end"
- **Universal front end** — Uses a universal tire; different from the car-specific V5 front end for Magnet Traction
- When customers search "magnet traction" on the website, only two tire results appear

## SKU 432 / SKU 444 — Phantom Tires Issue
- SKU 432 "Phantoms" are listed tires for certain cars
- SKU 444 "black tires" are different — a customer received 444s instead of 432s
- When wrong tires are on a car, Dan's approach: either send correct tires or send replacement car and have customer return the incorrect one

## General Tire Guidance
- For any car type: navigate to Shop by Car Type → [car type] → Tires — "anything that's in here will fit"
- Dan's standard response: "Here's a link to all of the rear tires that we offer for the [car type]"
- If a tire is out of stock, recommend signing up for the **in-stock notifier** and note "we restock weekly"

## Sponge Tires for Outlaw
- Sponge tires come on Outlaw cars but **Viper does not sell them separately**
- These are **special order items** from their supplier
- Dan's response: "hey, they're special order. They're X amount of dollars. How many pair would you like?"
- Customers typically order multiple pairs
- Different from "super tires" which are a different product
