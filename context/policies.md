# Viper Scale Racing Policies

Source: Discovery call with Dan, 2026-04-06

## Shipping
- **Free shipping on orders over $50**
- Many customers don't know about this threshold
- Customers frequently miss free shipping by small amounts (e.g., $0.25 short)
- Dan wants a system to alert customers: "spend another $X and you'll save $6 because we get free shipping at 50 bucks"

## Returns / Warranty
- Defective products: customer sends back to **"attention repairs"** at the shop mailing address
- Dan personally evaluates returned items
- For wrong parts on cars: Viper sends replacement and may ask for return of incorrect item
- 50-lap car failure example: Dan said that car "will go like 10,000 laps before it will physically just wear out" — 50 lap failure means something is different
- **Dan will still take care of customer even if they caused damage, but will let them know he knows**
- Never promise a specific warranty resolution before inspection

## Out-of-Stock Items
- **In-stock notifier** available on product pages
- "We restock weekly"
- Customers can build out their cart and wait for items to come back in stock
- If an alternative exists, suggest it along with the in-stock notifier

## Payment Methods
- Standard online checkout (credit cards)
- **Klarna** and **Affirm** financing available (customers sometimes can't get approved)
- **Direct invoicing** available as alternative for payment issues
- Deposit arrangements possible for large orders (tracks) — case by case, not advertised

## Customer Communication
- Primary channel: email (support@viperscaleracing.com)
- Phone: shop phone often goes unanswered, voicemails pile up
- Facebook messages: customers also reach out via Facebook
- Contact form: submits to email but groups multiple inquiries into single threads (problematic)
- Dan prefers email over phone for documentation purposes

## Third-Party Support Boundaries
- Viper does NOT provide support for third-party software/OS issues (e.g., Windows S-mode)
- For OS issues: provide a link to the fix but don't troubleshoot
- For timing system software: link to the vendor's tech support page and documentation
