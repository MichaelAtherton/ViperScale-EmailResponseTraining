## Current Workflow
1. All emails come into support@viperscaleracing.com inbox
2. Abby triages — answers what she can, puts rest in "pending" folder for Dan
3. Dan reviews pending folder (tries to do daily, sometimes falls behind)
4. For items Abby can't answer: she asks Dan for "cliffs notes" answer, then writes full response
5. Contact form submissions group into threads (problematic — multiple customers in one thread)
6. Order confirmations should go to Mailchimp "add to mailer" folder (461 backlog)
